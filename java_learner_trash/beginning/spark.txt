This project would try to provide an alternative to paper making process by suggesting an entirely different technique and prevent the reckless cutting of trees.


This would particularly help those people in rural places who are fortunate enough to have rivers near but unfortunate to have paper industries flushing their wastes into it.  On the other side, saving forests would save mankind.
